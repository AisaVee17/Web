About Laoag City

Laoag City, the capital of Ilocos Norte, is a vibrant destination filled with history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Sunshine City," it is home to stunning landmarks, rich traditions, and warm hospitality. From historic churches to sand dunes, Laoag offers something for every traveler.

Attractions

Tobacco Monopoly Monument

This monument serves as a homage to the Ilocano tobacco farmers.

La Paz Sand Dunes

Experience thrilling adventures like sandboarding and 4x4 rides in this unique desert landscape.

Sinking Bell Tower

One of the oldest and tallest bell towers in the Philippines, rich with history and mystery.
